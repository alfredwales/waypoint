How Smart Import Works

Your statement's imported. Here's what Waypoint just did behind the scenes — and what it'll keep doing on every import from now on.

🧠
Learns your categories
Categorise a transaction once, and Waypoint remembers that vendor — future imports from them get categorised automatically.
🔁
Applies categories in bulk
Set a category on one row and, if other transactions from the same vendor appear in this import, you'll be offered to apply it to all of them at once.
📅
Detects recurring bills
Transactions matching a bill you've already set up are automatically excluded, so it's never counted twice.
↔
Flags internal transfers
Money moved between your own accounts is marked as a transfer and left out of your income/expense totals.
